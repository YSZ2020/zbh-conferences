  • How to make payment to the hotel?

    You do not need to send your hotel payment along with the conference registration fee.When you leave the hotel, you will pay the expenses by cash or credit card.

  • Are there any excursions planned during the conference? 

    During the conference, we do not have travel plan. However, if you want to have sightseeing in China, we will help you to find a travel agency.

  • Is there any programme for spouses who might accompany?

    The program for participants' spouses is not finalized yet. If your spouse will accompany, we can book one room for you two and pick up both of you from the airport. However, you still should pay additional costs for your spouse (e.g. meals' expenses, etc.). The additional costs depend on your plan. Does your spouse want to participate in our conference? Or just want to attend meals with you? Please let us know your plan.
