Important Deadlines:

1.Conference abstracts are due on Oct. 20, 2014. The deadline for  notification of acceptance is Oct. 30, 2014. The deadline of full papers is Nov. 15, 2014.

2.Submission mailbox: IFATL2014@163.com

Requirements for conference papers:

1.The types of paper may include empirical research, conceptual analysis, poster presentations, and exhibition.

2.The abstract should include the title, author name(s), organization, and e-mail address. File name should be in the format of “IFATL2014+abstract+organzation+name”, File name for full paper might be in the format of “IFATL2014+full+organzation+name”.

Submissions should be unpublished papers.

The forum accepts the following three forms of contributions: (1) long papers(8 pages or so),(2) short articles (4 pages or so),(3) poster papers (2).
