Registration Time: 8:00- 22:00, Nov. 20, 2014（Beijing Time, GMT+8）；
Registration Location: Qixiayuan Hotel in Yanta Campus of Shaanxi Normal University
Address: No. 199. South Chang’an Road, Yanta District, Xi’an, Shaanxi.
