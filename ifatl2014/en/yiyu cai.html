Speaker：Yiyu Cai

Report Topic

When VR Meets Special Needs Education

Abstract

Virtual Reality (VR) was not new. In the 1960s, Ivan Sutherland pioneered the VR research by inventing the world first head-mounted display (HMD). Oculus is a HMD marketed by Facebook recently. In this talk, I will discuss some prior art of the VR research relevant to special needs education. I will then share some of my thought about VR and special needs, autistic spectrum disorder (ASD) in particular. I will present some the VR projects currently undergoing in my project team for special needs education.
